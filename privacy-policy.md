
# Privacy Policy for GPNR

**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

GPNR (Global Pi News Room) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within the Pi Network ecosystem.

### 1. Information Collection
GPNR is a news aggregation service. We do not require users to provide personal identification information (such as real names, addresses, or phone numbers) to access news content.

### 2. Use of Data
Any data processed by GPNR is used strictly for:
* Providing and improving the AI-driven news delivery service.
* Enhancing user experience within the 17 news categories.
* Ensuring technical stability of the app.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. All interactions occur within the secure Pi Browser environment.

### 4. Third-Party Links
Our service may contain links to external news sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites.

### 5. Consent
By using GPNR, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
